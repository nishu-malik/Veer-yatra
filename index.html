Upcurve

UpCurve Business Services Pvt Ltd. is an online air ticketing platform for the Indian Armed Forces, Paramilitary, Ex-servicemen and their dependents.

Our journey started in 2012, aggregating defence fares on our website www.udChalo.com. We soon realized that a number of troops were not able to avail our services due to unavailability of internet or reluctance to make online payments. In areas like Srinagar, Leh and North-East India internet connectivity is often a problem. So we decided to bring our services one step closer to our soldiers by establishing Air Ticket Booking Counters pan India.

Our first counter came up in BEG Pune in Jan, 2017. We now have more than 50 booking counters pan India.
